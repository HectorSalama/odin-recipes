Watermelon agua fresca!

Description

This drink is a simple combination of fresh watermelon, sugar, lime juice and water that gets blended together for a sweet and refreshing complement to the bold flavors of the dip.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 4 slices lime
  • 24 fresh mint leaves
  • ice

Steps

  1. Puree the watermelon and water in a blender until smooth.
  2. Add sugar to taste.
  3. Cut the lime slices in half.
  4. Place a half lime slice into each of 8 glasses along with 3 mint leaves.
  5. Crush with a cocktail muddler, then fill each glass with ice.
  6. Pour in the watermelon agua fresca, and stir before serving.
